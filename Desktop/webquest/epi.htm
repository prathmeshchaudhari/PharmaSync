Epilogue

Ay finally found out who the murderer was, it was Sennedjem, the teacher of Tutankhamun. Sennedjem when asked what the reason of killing was, said that he was jealous of Tutankhamun being crowned the king at a very young age. Sennedjem was sentenced to death. Ay took over the throne with great pride and thus everything ended in a peaceful manner.
