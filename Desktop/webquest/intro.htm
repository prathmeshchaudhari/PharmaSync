The Story Of Tutankhamun And Ay

Tutankhamun was an Egyptian pharaoh(king) of the
	 18th dynasty(1332-1323BC)
     He was crowned the king at an early age of 9
     Due to this he had attracted many haters
     In 1323BC a news was spread that he died
     Someone had killed him 
     His last words to his succesor were 
     Dear Ay
     I'll give you the path to find my murderer
     Find him and make sure that he is punished
     Ay was determined to find the murderer so
     he began the quest right away
